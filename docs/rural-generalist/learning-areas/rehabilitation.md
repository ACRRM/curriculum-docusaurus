- stroke 
- traumatic brain injury (TBI) 
- spinal injuries 
- amputations 
- arthritis, osteoarthritis 
- osteoporosis 
- cardiac disease
- respiratory 
- sensory loss 
- chronic pain
- cerebral palsy
- chronic degenerative conditions 
- neurogenic bladder and bowel conditions
- skin conditions including burns, scars and keloids
